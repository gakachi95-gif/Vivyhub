About us

Connecting the world, one call at a time

VivyHub is the team behind Vivy — a social calling platform built to make real conversation rewarding again.

Our mission

We believe genuine conversation shouldn't be rare. Most social platforms optimize for scrolling, not talking — so we built Vivy to bring back live, human connection through audio and video calls, and to make that connection valuable for the people who show up every day. Our mission is simple: help people meet, chat, and earn, without friction.

Our vision

We're building toward a future where hosting on Vivy is a legitimate, flexible career path — not a side hustle with a ceiling. That means continuing to invest in fair earnings, fast payouts, and tools that help hosts and agencies grow sustainably, wherever they are in the world. We want Vivy to be the platform people recommend when someone asks, "how do I actually make money from home?"

Our story

Vivy started with a simple observation: millions of people wanted to talk to someone new, and millions of others wanted a flexible way to earn from their time and personality. Existing calling apps solved half of that problem at best. We set out to build the platform that solved both sides at once — fast matching for users, and real, transparent income for hosts.

What began as a small team testing random-match calling in a handful of cities has grown into a platform used across more than 40 countries, with an agency system that lets entrepreneurs build entire businesses recruiting and supporting hosts. We're still early. The team behind VivyHub is small, product-obsessed, and focused on one thing: making every call on Vivy worth having.

What we stand for

  • Transparency — hosts always know exactly what they're earning and why.
  • Safety — every account is verified, and moderation runs around the clock.
  • Speed — from matching to payouts, we optimize for getting out of your way.
  • Fair earnings — hosts and agencies keep a meaningful share of what they generate.
